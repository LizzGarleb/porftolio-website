ABACROP
Abacrop is a digital agricultural data capture platform that helps farmers improve their productivity and efficiency. It is designed to be easy to use and affordable, making it accessible to small and medium-sized farmers. Abacrop provides farmers with access to weather data, market prices, and other agricultural information.

The Holberton School Foundation Course is an immersive program designed to provide students with a strong foundation in software engineering. It covers a wide range of topics, including full-stack development, algorithms, and system engineering, through hands-on, project-based learning. This self-paced program allows students to build a portfolio of impressive projects and prepares them for more advanced coursework and careers in the tech industry.
